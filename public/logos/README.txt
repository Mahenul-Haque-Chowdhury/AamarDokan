Place your payment provider logos in this folder with the following filenames:

- bkash.png   (recommended square PNG, ~128x128 or 256x256)
- nagad.png
- rocket.png
- upay.png

These are referenced by the checkout page at /src/app/checkout/page.tsx via Next/Image using /logos/<name>.png paths.

Tip: Transparent background PNGs look best on both light and dark backgrounds.
